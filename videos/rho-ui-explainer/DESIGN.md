# Rho UI Explainer Design

## Style Prompt

Use Rho's existing LiveView interface as the visual source: a warm ivory editorial workspace with compact panels, precise borders, copper activity accents, slate-blue secondary state, and dense but calm operational UI. The video should feel like the product itself is being guided and revealed, not like a separate marketing page.

## Colors

- Background abyss: `#F6F4F0`
- Deep surface: `#EDEAE4`
- Shelf/surface: `#FAF9F7`, `#FFFFFF`
- Ink text: `#1C1917`
- Secondary text: `#57534E`
- Muted text: `#A8A29E`
- Border: `#E2DFD8`
- Copper accent: `#C2855A`
- Copper strong: `#B07640`
- Slate secondary: `#7C8DB5`
- Success: `#6B9E78`
- Warning: `#C9A84C`
- Error: `#C75C54`

## Typography

- Body/interface: DM Sans, falling back to system sans
- Monospace metadata: JetBrains Mono, falling back to SF Mono or monospace

## Motion

- Use restrained product walkthrough motion: panel slides, focus rings, callout pins, cursor paths, row highlights, and subtle scale changes.
- Keep the UI legible at all times. Avoid excessive blur, spin, particle effects, or decorative blobs.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not introduce a dark tech palette or neon gradient identity.
- Do not use generic dashboard cards unrelated to Rho's chat/workspace split.
- Do not create a landing page hero instead of showing the actual app surfaces.
- Do not use oversized text inside compact app panels.
