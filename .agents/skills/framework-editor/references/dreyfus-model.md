# Dreyfus Proficiency Model

Default proficiency model for competency frameworks. 5 levels with Bloom's taxonomy action verbs.

## Level 1 — Novice (Foundational)

Follows established procedures. Needs supervision for non-routine situations. Recognizes basic concepts and terminology.

**Verbs:** identifies, follows, recognizes, describes, lists, recalls, names, defines

**Indicator pattern:** "Follows [process/procedure] with guidance. Identifies [basic concepts]. Recognizes when to escalate."

## Level 2 — Advanced Beginner (Developing)

Applies learned patterns to real situations. Handles routine tasks independently. Beginning to see similarities across situations.

**Verbs:** applies, demonstrates, executes, implements, operates, uses, performs, carries out

**Indicator pattern:** "Applies [method/technique] to routine [situations]. Demonstrates [skill] independently for standard cases."

## Level 3 — Competent (Proficient)

Plans deliberately. Organizes work systematically. Takes ownership of outcomes. Can prioritize and make trade-offs.

**Verbs:** analyzes, organizes, prioritizes, troubleshoots, coordinates, manages, plans, resolves

**Indicator pattern:** "Analyzes [complex situations] and prioritizes [actions]. Troubleshoots [issues] without guidance. Coordinates [work] across [scope]."

## Level 4 — Advanced (Senior)

Exercises judgment in ambiguous situations. Mentors others. Optimizes processes. Sees the big picture intuitively.

**Verbs:** evaluates, mentors, optimizes, integrates, influences, coaches, designs, leads

**Indicator pattern:** "Evaluates [trade-offs] in ambiguous situations. Mentors [others] on [skill]. Optimizes [processes] across [broader scope]."

## Level 5 — Expert (Master)

Innovates and shapes the field. Operates intuitively. Recognized authority. Creates new approaches and standards.

**Verbs:** architects, transforms, pioneers, establishes, strategizes, innovates, shapes, defines

**Indicator pattern:** "Architects [systems/approaches] that set [organizational/industry] standards. Pioneers [new methods]. Recognized as authority on [domain]."

## Usage Rules

- Each level MUST be distinct — non-overlapping with adjacent levels
- Use 2-3 verbs from the level's verb list per indicator
- Include scope/context that increases with each level (task → team → org → industry)
- Default to 5 levels unless user specifies otherwise
- Level names can be customized (the numbered levels are the constants)
