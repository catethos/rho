# CN Market Scan — PG18 BL Visual Novel Launch Assessment

**Scope**: PG18/车/R-18 Chinese-language (Simplified + diaspora) danmei market for a ~2-hour PG18 BL VN demo with CN+KR hybrid anime art. Market-first, premise-agnostic. One of four parallel scans (EN/CN/JP/KR).
**Compiled**: April 2026. Solo founder context (Regina, Malaysia-based).

---

## 1. Post-2021 Censorship Reality — Where PG18 Danmei Legally Exists (Nowhere, Honestly)

The legal environment for 中文 PG18 BL in 2026 is the worst it has ever been. Four compounding shocks:

**(a) 清朗 ("Clear and Bright") campaign, ongoing since 2021.** Cyberspace Administration of China (CAC) launched annual 清朗 specials targeting fanfic, 耽改, and "vulgar" content. Idol-養成 variety shows and 耽改剧 (BL-adapted dramas) were banned outright in Jan 2022. Post-2022 mainland "双男主剧" (bromance dramas) are *deliberately desexualized* to pass 广电 review. No licensed PG18 BL has aired on mainland streaming since.

**(b) AO3 blocked 29 Feb 2020** following the 227/Xiao Zhan incident. On 26 Feb 2020, fans of actor 肖战 mass-reported a Falling (《下坠》) RPF that reimagined him as a trans prostitute. Three days later CAC blocked AO3 on the mainland. AO3 has never been restored. VPN-only access since.

**(c) The Haitang Incident — this is the defining 2024–2025 event.** 海棠文学城 (Taiwan-hosted, Simplified-language danmei + erotica site) writers were targeted by **mainland cross-provincial police** (跨省抓捕 / so-called "远洋捕捞" — "deep-sea fishing"):

- **Phase 1 (June 2024, Anhui)**: 50+ authors detained on 制作传播淫秽物品牟利罪. Top author 云间 (Yunjian) sentenced to **4 years 6 months** + ¥1.8458M fine for 37 works earning ~¥1.85M on Haitang (Tencent News 6/20/2025; SCMP; Washington Blade 7/8/2025; RFA English 6/9/2025).
- **Phase 2 (H1 2025, Gansu/Lanzhou)**: Far broader sweep — 200–300 authors summoned across 20+ cities. Gansu police treated "free publication" and "micro-tips" as 牟利 (profit motive), dramatically expanding the offense. Chinese legal scholars publicly called this 趋利执法 (profit-motivated enforcement). Popular Weibo framing: "写黄文比强奸判得还重" ("writing erotica punished worse than rape").

**(d) 晋江 (Jinjiang) self-censorship deepened.** Jinjiang was named by CCTV + Zhejiang Consumer Protection Commission in Dec 2024 over "incomplete stories" (chapters mass-deleted post-censor). Jinjiang announced a formal 内容分级制 (content tiering) plan in 2024 — but in practice, any 车 gets deleted or locked behind V-chapters that writers self-censor before upload. 晋江 is now effectively SFW or near-SFW.

**Verdict for Q1**: **No legal onshore rail exists for PG18 Chinese-language BL in 2026.** Consumption happens entirely via (i) VPN to overseas platforms, (ii) grey-market mirror sites, and (iii) private groups. This is structurally important for the game question in §6.

## 5. Setting × PG18 — Is 古风 Still King?

**古风 is the most prestigious but 现代都市 moves more volume for PG18.**

- **古风 PG18**: Literary flagship (魔道祖师, 天官赐福 as reference points; both Seven Seas NYT bestsellers through 2025). Highest prestige, highest payoff, **hardest to do on 2-hour budget**.
- **现代都市 PG18**: Lowest friction, widest audience. 娱乐圈 is the single most saturated PG18 subgenre on JJWXC 2025 rankings.
- **玄幻 / 仙侠 PG18**: Strong but art/world budget-heavy.
- **无限流 PG18**: The **dark-horse winner for a 2-hour VN**. Praised as a chassis where "车 fits naturally" because each 副本 is self-contained. Low world-build per-minute cost, high novelty, naturally episodic — matches VN pacing.
- **ABO modern/scifi**: Setting multiplier on any of above.

**Answer**: For a solo-founder 2-hour demo, **无限流** or **modern 都市娱乐圈** beat 古风 on ROI. 古风 is a brand-maturity move, not a demo-1 choice.

## 6. CN BL *Game* Awareness — The Critical Question

**Do CN danmei fans play PG18 BL VNs? Overwhelmingly NO, with a small high-intent niche.**

Evidence:
- CnGal.org indexes BL VNs in a tiny sub-section. Zhihu 664954928 and 642890762 list ~10 BL VNs total worth mentioning — almost all **JP imports** (Slow Damage, Hashihime of the Old Book Town, Paradise, 祭品小镇, 旧书店街的桥姬). **No breakout domestic-CN-made PG18 BL VN exists.**
- 2024 JP-BL 官中 localization wave (3 titles in 2 months) signals pent-up **consumer demand** — but unit sales are likely low-5-figures at best.
- **Contrast with 女性向 mobile**: 恋与深空 did ¥58.7亿 (~$800M USD) global 2024, ~60% from China. 光与夜之恋 overtook it on iOS 女性向 in Nov 2025. Four-Major otome = 恋与深空 / 光与夜之恋 / 世界之外 / 时空中的绘旅人. **All het, none BL, none PG18.**
- **Domestic CN BL mobile games**: Effectively zero pass 版号 (game license) review. 2022 双男主 ban extended de facto to gachas.

**Translation**: CN female gamers spend big — but on **het otome gacha inside the Great Firewall**, not PG18 BL VNs outside it. The VN-playing CN danmei fan is a **diaspora + VPN-using otaku hybrid** — small, sophisticated, English-adjacent, often already reading JP/KR BL.

## 7. Love and Deepspace Recipe — What Transfers to PG18 BL

L&D = 3D character intimacy + LiveKiss + haptic + gacha + relationship ops. **$933.7M cumulative** through 2025, Y2 *up 27% YoY* over Y1 ($522.6M vs $410M). 60% China share.

**Transfers to PG18 BL VN:**
- **Intimacy-first marketing beats plot-first.** Key-art of "the moment" outperforms synopsis. Lean into 车 the way L&D leans into kisses.
- **Character-brand over IP-brand.** L&D sells Zayne / Xavier / Rafayel individually. A BL VN demo should elevate ONE love interest to brand-carrier status.
- **Short-loop emotional beats.** L&D hooks are sub-2min. A VN demo should deliver a full seduction arc (meet→tension→kiss→scene) inside 30min.
- **Polished Chinese VO is non-negotiable** if targeting CN diaspora. L&D set the floor.

**Does NOT transfer:**
- Gacha economy — too capital-intensive.
- 3D rendering — forfeit; CN+KR 2D art is correct.
- License-dependent distribution — L&D is 版号-approved; PG18 BL is *structurally locked out* of Apple App Store CN / TapTap / iQiyi / Douyin. Distribution is Steam + itch.io + direct downloads. Accept this.

## 8. PG18 Distribution Economics — Who Makes Money at What Volume

**Patreon (CN-diaspora creators)**: Fan-artist tiers $3/$5/$10/$20. Fiction-creator tiers rarer and lower ceiling. Top CN-language Patreon danmei accounts are translator-led, earning low-four-figures USD/month. **No confirmed 5-figure/month native CN-language PG18 BL creator on Patreon as of early 2026.**

**Ko-fi**: 0% on one-time tips, 5% on memberships. Favored by translators + artists. Low absolute volume per creator.

**爱发电**: RMB-native, post-2024 tightened 18+ restrictions. Most PG18 creators migrated off.

**Gumroad / itch.io (Simplified Chinese products)**: The itch.io zh_Hans page shows active but small market. A CN+EN bilingual PG18 BL VN on itch.io is **effectively un-tested territory** — no CN-native breakout. The only ~breakout danmei itch products are TTRPG pamphlets (danmeistory.itch.io). Opportunity is real because competition is thin.

**Steam (Simplified Chinese language support)**: The one truly scalable legal CN-language PG18 rail. Adult-filter + SC language + overseas payment. 2024 saw the JP BL 官中 wave demonstrate demand. **A Steam launch with SC+EN is the rational beachhead**, but expect 4-figure SC-language unit sales ceiling for a demo from a new studio, unless one post goes viral on XHS.

**Real punchline**: CN PG18 **creators** do not make tech-company money. Top tier = mid-5-figures USD/yr net; median serious creator = mid-4-figures USD/yr. **This does not get a Malaysia-based founder to Y1 ramen-profitable alone.**

## 9. Red Flags for Foreign / Overseas-Chinese Creators

**Gets you branded 哗众取宠 / 洋人写耽美 / not authentic:**
- Western yaoi aesthetic pretending to be CN (muscly-bear tops — reads as Euro bara, not 攻).
- Getting 攻受 cues visually wrong (subtle — body language, posture, eyeline).
- Pidgin Chinese or machine-translated text. Immediate disqualifier. Needs native 润色.
- Ignorance of 雷点 — writing 双性生子 without warning reads as hostile.
- K-BL webtoon CP-tropes imposed on CN aesthetic without hybrid justification.
- White-savior framing of "rescuing" CN creators post-Haitang. Condescending.
- Over-explanation of CN culture ("like Chinese Shakespeare!") — reads as writing *about* CN for outsiders, not *for* CN readers.

**Earns trust:**
- Tape-receipts: favorite novels, Lofter CPs, JJWXC history screenshots.
- Hiring a CN 润色 editor and crediting them.
- Native 笔名 without exoticizing.
- Heat written *to taste* (车味 / dirty-talk register matches CN BL conventions, not JP or EN).
- Respecting Haitang arrests contextually — silence-with-craft is fine; flippancy is not.
- Clean HE + 双洁 default unless aggressively signaled.
- **Regina's Malaysia-Chinese positioning is net-positive** (not mainland, not Western) — but only if craft is tight. Weak craft + diaspora framing = perceived as 圈外人.

## 10. Gap Analysis — Underserved PG18 BL Premises in Game Form for CN Diaspora

Cross-referencing §3 × §5 × §6 (near-zero domestic-CN-made BL VNs):

**Strong gaps (opportunity):**
1. **无限流 PG18 BL VN** — zero domestic-CN, zero English. Chassis is *designed* for episodic VNs. 2-hour demo = one 副本 = full scene arc including 车.
2. **现代娱乐圈 PG18 BL VN** — most-read BL subgenre on JJWXC has no significant VN adaptation. A "rising idol × cynical manager" demo with ~30min 车 scene would be recognizable-hot.
3. **ABO PG18 VN with CN-aesthetic art** (not JP-ABO-manga clone) — tight chassis, clear kink marketing, compatible with CN+KR hybrid pipeline.
4. **Xianxia-flavored 无限流 PG18** (cultivation-dungeon hybrid) — combines 古风 prestige with 无限流 efficiency. Untapped.

**Weak gaps (avoid — underserved because audience is thin):**
- Pure 古风 宫廷 PG18 VN — art budget + expectation gap kills solo projects.
- Hardcore BDSM / non-con as primary draw — payment rail fragile; reputation-fragile.
- Western-setting BL with CN MC — reads as 洋人写耽美.

## 11. Honest Verdict — Is CN Diaspora Your LAUNCH Market?

**Short answer: NO. CN diaspora is MARKET-2 or MARKET-3, not MARKET-1. Launch EN first (likely) or KR (possibly). Use CN as scale-out after validation.**

**Reasoning (unsentimental):**

1. **Rail mismatch.** CN PG18 creator-economy has no mature paywall rail in Chinese. Haitang radioactive. Jinjiang sanitized. Lofter unstable. 爱发电 tightening. Steam SC is the best single option but yields 4-figure SC unit sales on debut. **A solo founder cannot survive on CN-only debut revenue.**

2. **Game form is ~untested.** CN female gamers spend on het otome gachas inside GFW; CN BL VN buyers are a diaspora + VPN-otaku micro-niche, not a mass audience. Building a new rail and a new taste simultaneously = two unvalidated variables.

3. **Political risk is real.** Haitang arrests are not hypothetical — 50+ to 300 authors detained 2024–2025, some with 4+ year sentences. Malaysia-based Chinese creator is not directly in jeopardy, but any payment flow / mainland collaborator / server in Shenzhen introduces risk. Don't route money through mainland entities.

4. **Confirmation-bias check.** Regina leans CN personally. This is the *exact* scenario where founder taste and market reality diverge. CN fluency is an asset for QA + marketing + localization, not a reason to lead with CN.

5. **Comps — what actually broke out:**
   - **English-language danmei novels** (Seven Seas / MXTX) — NYT bestseller, ongoing 2025. Market is hot and undersupplied for game form.
   - **English-language Steam BL VNs** (Tennis Ace, Sentimental Trickster, Red Embrace) — small but real.
   - **KR webtoon BL** — Lezhin/Bomtoon paywall rails mature; PG18 acceptance higher; ARPPU higher. KR is a legitimate launch candidate if KR art pipeline is half of Regina's pipeline.

6. **Cautionary tales:**
   - Haitang authors (the literal ceiling of CN PG18 creator economy) are now imprisoned. Creators who built their careers there lost everything overnight.
   - Multiple 2023–24 Steam BL VNs with SC support (Paradise, 祭品小镇) achieved SC unit sales in low-4-figures — not enough to fund another project solo.

**Recommended sequencing:**
- **MARKET-1: EN** — Seven Seas readership primed for game form; AO3 crowd bridges; itch.io + Steam EN validated; rails mature.
- **MARKET-2: KR** (pending KR scan) — if KR webtoon rails absorb BL VN demos, revenue-per-reader beats EN.
- **MARKET-3: CN (diaspora, Simplified)** — ship SC localization of Demo 1 *simultaneously-but-unmarketed*; let XHS 推文 accounts discover it organically; use CN signal as proof-of-concept for CN-first Demo 2 only if signal is strong.

**What CN-diaspora IS good for right now (use, don't lead):**
- Cheap, high-quality XHS discovery engine. A well-made 9-image 推文 for Demo 1 in SC can break out for near-zero marketing cost.
- Chinese-language voice actors (from drama CD scene) available at Malaysia-accessible rates.
- CN+KR hybrid art aesthetic already aligned with diaspora taste.
- 无限流 premise chassis (§10) is culturally-CN-native but mechanically-universal — designed once, reads right in three markets.

**Final line**: Ship Demo 1 in EN + SC bilingual. Lead marketing in EN. Measure CN signal passively. Don't pad the roadmap to reassure founder's personal taste. If EN validates and CN signal is strong, CN-native Demo 2 becomes the obvious bet in 12 months. If CN signal is weak, you learned cheap.
