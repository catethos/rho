# W3 — Chapter 1 Plot Outline

**Project:** PG18 BL VN Demo 1 — Shanghai shikumen 副本
**Scope:** ~2 hours reading/gameplay. One 副本, one night, one loop. 子时 → 卯时.
**Status:** v0.1 decisive skeleton. Regina accepts, edits, or pushes back at the beat level.
**Companion docs:** W1 world bible, W1 research notes, W2 MC bible, W2 LI bible.

This outline is structural. It is not dialogue, not prose, not locked. Its job is to let W4 pick up scene 1 without re-reading the bibles. Framework grounding throughout: **Todorov** (equilibrium → disruption → new equilibrium), **Propp** (morphological functions, applied loosely), **Freytag with BL-VN adjustment** (climax is the 天井 speech-act, *not* the intimacy scene — the intimacy scene is the midpoint / false peak), **McKee** (inciting incident at 10–25% of demo; controlling idea above plot), **Barthes hermeneutic code** (every scene raises ≥1 question, resolves at most half of one).

---

## 1. Act Structure Overview

| Act | Scenes | 时辰 span | Controlling idea (one sentence) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Act I — The place that recognized him** | 1–4 | Pre-子时 → early 子时 (22:00–00:30) | A man who has been half-absent from his own life crosses a threshold he did not know was a threshold, and the neighborhood takes him as a tenant. |
| **Act II — Tenant by etiquette, resident by intimacy** | 5–9 | 丑时 → 寅时 (01:00–04:30) | To keep from being overwritten, he has to learn how to be seen — first by the neighbors, then by one person, then by himself. |
| **Act III — The sentence the dream will accept** | 10–13 | Late 寅时 → 卯时 / 破晓 (04:30–05:45) | The thing he has never said aloud is the only thing that opens the door; survival is indistinguishable from confession. |

Total: **13 scenes**. Scene 12 splittable into 12a/12b for pacing — optional, flagged below.
Demo word count ballpark (EN build, VN prose register, Ren'Py-tagged): **28,000–34,000 words**. At ~280 words/VN-minute with choice-branching overhead, this lands near the 2-hour target.

## 3. Choice Points

Per MC bible §1: **choices are character-defining, not plot-defining.** Four total. One is Bad-End-gating.

### Choice 1 — "Drink from the vat?" *(Scene 4)*
- **Between:** Drink (thirst) / Don't drink (caution).
- **Changes:** Anchor delta (–7% if drink, –3% if not). 大姆妈 in scene 6 reads MC slightly more/less suspiciously. No branching.
- **Character-defining:** Does MC *take* what looks offered to a tenant who looks like him, or wait to be invited? The whole wound compressed into a small decision. The player feels his reflex before they understand the mechanic.

### Choice 2 — "What to ask the tenant" *(Scene 7)*
- **Between:** (a) soft personal, (b) structural-rule, (c) silent thermos-offer.
- **Changes:** Which sliver of LI first. Minor anchor reinforcement on (c). No branching.
- **Character-defining:** Information, safety, or presence? Three valid MCs live in the bible; this picks which one the player drives through the middle of the night.

### Choice 3 — "The consent beat" *(Scene 9)*
- **Between:** Two-to-three consent-moments inside the intimacy scene (verbal yes / not-yet / specify).
- **Changes:** Scene 9 register and the aftercare beat. Slight anchor delta. No branching — all roads lead to successful braid. Refusing at this point pauses and re-asks; consent is the rule, not a gate.
- **Character-defining:** MC has spent years saying no by going silent. Saying yes aloud is the first real reverse of his withdrawal pattern. The choice *is* the reverse.

### Choice 4 — "天井, what to say" *(Scene 12)* — **HIGH-STAKES, BAD-END-GATING**
- **Between:** (a) true secret, true register; (b) hedge; (c) wrong-register substitute.
- **Changes:** The ending.
  - (a) → Good End.
  - (b) → Bad End B (diminished).
  - (c) → Bad End A (overwrite).
- **Function:** The one choice both character-defining *and* plot-defining. The demo's thesis — survival *is* honest speech in the right register — literalized. Player must have earned their read of MC to pick (a); the others are valid failure modes, not traps.

**Rationale on only 4 choices:** BL-VN Ch1 convention is 4–8. 4 is deliberately low. Fewer choices preserves the authored emotional arc and avoids the "illusion of agency" trap. When scope expands, 1/2/3 can proliferate; 4 must remain singular.

## 4. The Five Pivotal Scenes (expanded)

### (a) The threshold crossing — Scene 2

The player knows MC is entering a 副本 ten seconds before MC does. The scene is engineered for that asymmetry. Diegetically: MC is following 高德地图 into a 里弄 off 西康路 for a coworker's hard-drive handoff at a specific 亭子间 address. Non-diegetically: scene 1's voice work (withdrawal, sister, plant) has cued the player to recognize MC as the archetype the dream selects for. The threshold literalizes both vectors at once.

**Load-bearing gesture:** MC steps *on* the 门槛, not over it. One line. No commentary. CN audience decodes instantly; EN audience reads wrongness via register, paid off later when the rule is named. The neighbor-silhouette's nod confirms the weld.

**Narratologically:** this single beat does the work of Propp's *Departure*, McKee's *Inciting Incident*, and Todorov's *Disruption* simultaneously, in under 90 seconds of screen-time. Collapsing three functions into one image is how a 2-hour demo accommodates its form constraints.

**Barthes check:** questions posed — *what is this place? why does the neighbor recognize him? why is his phone dying?* Resolved: zero. Half-resolved: the place-type (it is a shikumen). Withheld: everything else. Within discipline.

**Writer's texture:** the walk from Line 2 to 西康路 (~8–12 sentences of §4 voice), the ambient cross-fade from modern Shanghai to the lane, the specific quality of 子时 lantern light. Do not signpost. Do not foreshadow through dread. Trust the image.

### (b) The 灶披间 etiquette save — Scene 6

Establishes LI's *social-etiquette competence* in a register the player has never seen a BL LI operate in. Not combat, not supernatural, not romantic — a man, a neighbor, a 大姆妈, a stool, a thermos pattern, three steps sideways, done so cleanly the save is invisible to everyone except the player.

**Load-bearing components:**
1. **"大姆妈"** — Shanghainese, not standard. CN readers hear the specificity; EN readers feel the weight without parsing. Gloss once in MC's interior, italic thereafter.
2. **Thermos compliment — "这个花样现在买不到了罢"** — social-engineering payload. True sentence about the *object*, absorbs 大姆妈's attention while redirecting MC. No false flattery. The dream does not metabolize invention (W1 §7).
3. **Three steps sideways without touching** — careful, not controlling. LI's hands do not come near MC.
4. **"It's — I am doing what the room is for"** — half-hermeneutic. Raises *what is the room for.* Resolves nothing.

**Narratologically:** earliest deployment of **Propp's Donor function in compromised form** — LI gives MC the *effect* of a magical agent (rescue) without the *content* (he does not tell MC what rule was broken). Seeds the unreliable-mentor reading: helpful, yes; forthcoming, no. Every later trust-building beat has to climb against this seed.

**Writer's texture:** 大姆妈's face and register (not described in bibles — writer picks). Save-length shorter than reader expects (90–120 seconds of screen-time). The re-settling silence afterward.

### (c) The 荒鸡 / LI's supernatural competence — Scene 8

The only scene in Ch1 where the supernatural is **named by action** rather than implied by register. Everywhere else the wrongness is ambient; here it is event. Scoped tight — shown once, at cost, without explanation.

**Load-bearing components:**
1. **The 荒鸡 cry** — CN audience decodes instantly (rule-violation marker, research §6); EN audience reads it as wrong-time-wrong-sound. Both land.
2. **LI's walk to the center** — unhurried. Most-capable-entity posture.
3. **The palm on the lintel, from the inside** — the inversion of his shadow tell (his shape *is* the lintel; touching the lintel is touching himself). Player does not decode on first read; re-player does.
4. **The shadow reveal** — lighting angle is why it appears *here*, not earlier. Before scene 8 the light has been wrong; here it is right. MC sees once, without commentary; §4 voice logs it without articulating; reader decodes in the margin.
5. **LI's cost** — paleness, slower focus, half-beat eye delay. He paid. Does not say what.

**Narratologically:** **Propp's Struggle + Branding** compressed into one move. LI does not *fight* the violation; he *absorbs* it. And the branding is on him, not on MC — paleness, slower eyes. Propp's standard morphology puts the brand on the hero. Here the Donor takes it. Thematically significant: LI is *always* the one paying for MC's presence, the shape of the larger romance across future demos.

**What the scene does NOT do:** explain. LI's *"It was the building remembering a mistake. I settled it."* is true and closed. No exposition dump. The player leaves with one piece of supernatural confirmed, one tell visible, one debt opened.

**Writer's texture:** quality of MC's freeze (§4 register tightens audibly); ambient shift of the 天井 (vat going wrongly still is specified; everything else is the writer's); the silence after.

### (d) The mid-dungeon intimacy scene — Scene 9

**What the scene is FOR** (not what it *does* choreographically):

1. **To execute consent-as-mechanic on page.** Verbal beats are not decoration; they are the physics. The whole demo's ethics stands or falls here.
2. **To perform "the ritual of asking the question that has no other language."** LI cannot ask MC *"are you the one who can answer for me?"* and MC cannot ask LI *"are you a man?"* Intimacy is the register where the question is posed without being verbalized. The scene does not answer it; it establishes *the question has a shape, and the shape is MC.*
3. **To reverse MC's withdrawal pattern once, under controlled pressure.** MC has spent three years saying no by going silent. For 45–60 minutes of screen-time he is a person who says yes out loud. The reversal is the point. The rest of the demo is about whether the reversal survives dawn.
4. **To land the 辛苦侬了 moment.** The scene's fracture. LI says a phrase his mother said to his father, has not heard himself say in decades. Both still. Emotional peak is *not* a climax of arousal; it is this sentence.
5. **To deliver the cloth-fold aftercare.** Ten minutes of quiet repetition. MC takes the cloth. LI releases it. The *taking* is the recovery — not the sex.

**What the scene is NOT for:**
- Arousal as spectacle.
- A test MC can fail.
- A reveal of LI's nature (shadow already in scene 8; body-warmth tell here is texture, not plot beat).
- A choice-gate (Choice 3 shapes register; it does not branch).

**Discipline:** explicit on its own terms — bodies, specifics, adult language, fluids allowed. *Every physical beat must be psychological.* If a line of choreography does not double-duty, cut.

**Narratologically:** Freytag's midpoint / false-peak by BL-VN pacing convention. The reader will *feel* this as the climax; the form teaches otherwise in scene 12. Deliberate calibration — the rebound propels Act III.

### (e) The 天井 climax — Scene 12

The dream's contradiction (W1 §1) is solved not by confession but by **translation.** MC does not speak his secret; he translates it out of 2026 withdrawal-register into **the grammar of a domestic confession his family did not receive in 2022.**

**Locked SC:**
> *"我没去奶奶的丧礼。不是因为工作。我是怕他们问我。——奶奶，我对不起您,我到现在还在不敢。"*

**EN gloss (writer localizes per §7):**
> *"I didn't go to Nai-nai's funeral. Not because of work. Because I was afraid of what they would ask. — Nai-nai, I'm sorry. I'm still, even now, afraid."*

**Why this works for the dream's grammar:**
1. **奶奶** — kinship term specific to paternal grandmother, not 外婆, not generic 祖母. Dream's logic is domestic; word is domestic.
2. **"不是因为工作"** — the small lie, publicly retracted in the 天井 where a household's small lies used to get caught.
3. **"我是怕他们问我"** — the real reason, unglossed. No explanation of *what* they would ask. The dream needs the *shape* of the admission, not the content.
4. **"我对不起您"** — formal apology register (您). 1990s–2010s domestic-confession grammar. Not Weibo-modern, not classical. The register his grandmother would have accepted at a kitchen table.
5. **"到现在还在不敢"** — present-continuous cowardice. Confession is *not* past. He is still afraid *now*. What makes the dream accept — the confession includes its own ongoing unresolution. The dream does not demand redemption; it demands honesty about the *shape of the thing.*

**Narratologically:** the **anagnorisis** (Aristotelian recognition) — MC recognizes what he has been doing to himself for three years, in the act of speaking it. Simultaneously the **peripeteia** — not of external fortune but of his withdrawal pattern. And Freytag's climax, structurally distinct from the false peak (scene 9) because this beat discharges the **controlling idea** (honesty-as-survival), whereas scene 9 discharged the romantic arc's false resolution.

**What LI does during this scene:** nothing visible. He is in the 灶披间, off-page, listening. The demo does not cut to him. **The writer must not cut to him.** MC must be alone in the 天井 because the act of honest speech must be unwitnessed-in-the-frame for the dream to accept it — the addressee is the grandmother, not the LI. If the camera cuts to LI, the scene becomes a relationship beat instead of a speech-act. Do not cut.

## 5. Bad End Mapping

### Bad End A — Overwrite (primary, locked in-scope)

- **Branch point:** Scene 12, Choice 4 path (c) — MC speaks something true but wrong-register (work grievance, professional confession).
- **Trigger logic:** the dream cannot metabolize the wrong register; anchor hits 0%; weld completes.
- **Final scene (~80 words):** The 天井. Dawn still arrives; MC does not notice. A bowl of rice porridge cooling on the 亭子间 table. MC is there, setting out a second. A knock at the 石库门 — a new runner, tired, phone dying, lantern-light behind him. MC opens the door. Says, softly, in a register that is not quite his own: *"你来了。别喝水缸里的水,那是给明早预备的。"* His ring is gone. The tenant is in the 天井, watching. Both of them know. Neither says. Tender-horror. Fade.

### Bad End B — Diminished (bonus scope, cuttable)

- **Branch point:** Scene 12, Choice 4 path (b) — hedge.
- **Trigger logic:** the dream accepts the hedge enough to open the door but the anchor has dropped to ~35%. MC exits incomplete.
- **Final scene (~80 words):** MC's real apartment, a week later. He is on the phone with his sister. He is trying to ask about her husband. He cannot remember the man's name. She is quiet on the line. "哥哥," she says, "你是不是太累了。" He says he is fine. He says he will call her back. He puts down the phone. The dead pothos still on the windowsill. He opens the voicemail app. The voicemail from April 4 is still unplayed. He does not play it. He cries, small and unshowy, and does not know why.
- **Cut-criteria:** ship A only; leave B wireframed for Demo 2 integration. The demo does not *need* B for its thesis.

### Bad End C — Loop compression (bonus scope, cuttable, hardest to reach)

- **Branch point:** *Not* Choice 4. Triggered by anchor dropping below 40% *before* scene 12 — via accumulated etiquette failures (drank vat in 4, sat on 门槛 in 5 despite warning, photographed the 天井 with phone in 8, aggressively pressed the forbidden question in 10). Multiple failures required — not reachable on a single bad choice.
- **Trigger logic:** dawn arrives; contradiction unsolved; the 副本 does not dissolve — it **compresses.** Loop begins, shorter.
- **Final scene (~80 words):** Scene 2 replayed. MC crosses the 石库门 threshold again. This time the lane lantern is dimmer, the neighbor-silhouette does not nod, MC's phone shows *05:43, Friday morning* — the time he should have been leaving. MC does not know this is the second loop. The tenant, in the 天井, does. The tenant looks toward the 石库门 and, for the first time, almost speaks MC's name. Cannot. Cliffhanger. Seeds Demo 2+.
- **Cut-criteria:** cuttable. A single-line hint of compression in scene 10 ("the night runs the same way every time") covers the seed without needing the branch.

**Ship recommendation:** Good End + Bad End A. B and C are Demo 2 material.

**Information asymmetry rotation:** favors the player for the first third (scenes 1–5), balances by scene 8, then *flips* in the 天井 climax — MC knows what he is saying; the player has to piece together why the register matters. This rotation is the demo's sophistication.

## 7. Register Calibration for Bilingual Ship

**Baseline:** most scenes play identically. MC's voice is already engineered for both (MC bible §5). One draft localizes beat-for-beat for 80–85%.

**Mandatory divergences:**

1. **Scene 6 — "大姆妈"** — honorific does not translate. **Italic untranslated on first use, one-line interior gloss** (*"the kind of auntie-neighbor older than the stove"*), then italic-only on subsequent uses. Preserves linguistic texture without footnote UI.

2. **Scene 9 — "辛苦侬了"** — **the load-bearing Shanghainese line.** Recommendation: **italic untranslated on page, MC's interior gloss** — *"a phrase in a register that was not his — older, softer, Shanghainese somewhere — a thing said to someone at the end of a day they survived."* Do NOT translate to "thank you for your hard work" (kills register) or "thanks for carrying all that" (too casual). The beat is that MC does not understand the words and understands the *sentence shape.* Italic + interior-context preserves this. **Flagged for Regina's sign-off.**

3. **Scene 10 — "You crossed in as if you had been here"** — lands in EN at full strength. In SC it also carries a class-and-tenancy undertone from 过 that EN cannot. No intervention; SC readers get a bonus; EN readers get the core.

4. **Scene 12 — the climactic sentence.** SC locked as *"我没去奶奶的丧礼..."* **EN must not be a direct translation.** "Grandma" is the register-equivalent of 奶奶; "grandmother" is too formal. "Funeral" is fine; "wake" is too Western-Catholic. The pivotal grief-word in SC is 奶奶; in EN it should be the proper-noun **"Nai-nai"**, with one-time gloss earlier in Act I (recommended: scene 1 or 3, MC silently sees a photograph of her in his apartment, interior caption identifies her as *"Nai-nai, 1946–2022"*). EN climactic line reads *"I didn't go to Nai-nai's funeral..."* at full grief-register. **Structural localization decision — Regina signs off.**

5. **Scene 13 — the final line.** *"That's all right. You will next time."* / *"不要紧。下次你会知道的。"* Equivalent weight. EN carries a slight melancholic-future register; SC carries a slight reassurance register. Both correct. No intervention.

6. **MC's anchor-degradation interior** — article-dropping in EN, 武汉 rhythm return in SC. Parallel-but-different degradation cues. Writer calibrates per-scene, not auto-localized.
